Substance Use(Medicated Assisted Treatment).

Our Substance Use Services offer compassionate, nonjudgmental care for individuals and families navigating challenges with alcohol, prescription medications or other substances. We believe recovery is possible for everyone, and we partner with you to build a healthier, more empowered future.

Our Approach?

We center every treatment plan on your unique strengths, goals and life circumstances.

Personalized care plans grounded in evidence-based therapies.

Collaborative goal-setting with you and your support network.

Integration with primary care, mental health counseling and wellness resources.

Services Offered

Comprehensive Assessment & Screening

A detailed evaluation to understand your history, patterns and readiness for change.

Individual Counseling

One-on-one sessions using motivational interviewing, cognitive behavioral therapy and relapse prevention strategies.

Group Therapy

Peer-support groups led by licensed clinicians to foster connection, accountability and shared learning.

Medication-Assisted Treatment(MAT)

Safe administration of FDA-approved medications alongside counseling to reduce cravings and withdrawal symptoms.

Family Support & Education

Workshops and counseling for loved ones to strengthen communication, set boundaries and promote healing for the whole family.

Aftercare & Relapse Prevention

Ongoing support plans, check-ins and community referrals to ensure sustainable recovery.

Telehealth Options

Virtual appointments for assessment, counseling and medication management - accessible from home or work.

Who We Serve

Adults seeking to regain control over substance use.

Adolescents and young adults navigating early intervention.

Families and partners looking for education and healing tools.

Individuals with co-occurring mental health concerns.

Ready to Take the Next Step?

Recovery begins the moment you decide to ask for help. Contact our Substance Use Services team today to learn more or schedule your first appointment.
