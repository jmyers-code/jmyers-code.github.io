Protect Your Health Before You Go - and After You Return

Whether you're heading overseas for vacation, business, mission work or study abroad, our Travel Medicine provides personalized care to help you travel safely and stay well. We offer trusted guidance, vaccinations and medications tailored to your destination and travel plans.

What We Offer

Pre-Travel Consultations

Personalized risk assessments and advice based on your itinerary, health status and planned activities.

Vaccinations and Preventive Care

Includes Yellow Fever, Typhoid, Hepatitis A & B, MMR and other region-specific immunizations.

Travel Medications

Prescriptions for malaria prevention, altitude sickness, motion sickness, traveler's diarrhea and more.

Post-Travel Checkups

If you return with health concerns - including fever, gastrointestinal issues or fatigue - we're here to help.

Who Should Visit

International travelers visiting countries with different health risks.

Students studying abroad.

Missionary or volunteer travelers.

Families planning multi-country vacations.

Business travelers needing fast, reliable care.

What Sets Us Apart:

Fast Appointments and flexible scheduling.

Expert Guidance from caring medical providers.

Certified Yellow Fever Site (if applicable).

Comprehensive Service: travel, wellness and routine care - all in one place.
