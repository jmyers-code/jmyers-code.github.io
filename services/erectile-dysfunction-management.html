What Is Erectile Dysfunction?

Erectile Dysfunction(ED) is the consistent inability to achieve or maintain an erection firm enough for sexual activity. It's more common than many realize and can result from a variety of factors, including:

Chronic conditions like diabetes, hypertension or heart disease.

Hormonal imbalances or low testosterone.

Psychological stress, anxiety or depression.

Lifestyle factors such as smoking, alcohol use or poor sleep.
