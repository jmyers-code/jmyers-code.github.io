About Us

The New Creatures’ Fellowship (NCF) is the main affiliate church of
the Final Global Movement and its mission is to fuel God’s final,
fastest and widest move on the earth before the Rapture.

The New Creatures’ Fellowship (NCF) is a worldwide network of churches
presided over by the highly esteemed Dr. David Bindan headquartered in Accra, Ghana.
The esteemed Rev. Dr. Felix Kingsley Opuni is the head pastor. The Fellowship is the
main affiliate church of the Final Global Movement and its mission is to fuel God’s final,
fastest and widest move on the earth before the Rapture. You are welcome to fellowship
with any of our assemblies near you this and every Sunday. We would love to have you with us.
