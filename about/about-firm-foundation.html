About Firm Foundation LLC

Firm Foundation, LLC, established in 2024 by Dr. Agyenim Amoako and Patience Agbeviadey, is a premier provider of integrated medical and mental health support services in Sussex County, Delaware. We are committed to delivering exceptional, holistic care in a single, convenient location, ensuring seamless coordination and continuity of care. Our approach is grounded in personalized care plans tailored to each patient's unique needs, promoting overall well-being and optimal health outcomes.
